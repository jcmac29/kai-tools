Pro Tips for Better AI Prompts
  • Be specific about the format you want (bullet points, paragraphs, table, etc.)
  • Include context about your audience and their knowledge level
  • Specify the tone: professional, casual, friendly, authoritative
  • Ask the AI to take on a specific role or persona for better results
  • Include examples of what you want when possible
