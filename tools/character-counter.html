About Character Counter

This free character counter tool helps you count characters for social media posts, meta descriptions, and more. Click on any platform above to track your character count against that platform's limit.

The progress bar shows you exactly how much space you've used. Green means you're under the limit, yellow means you're getting close (80%+), and red means you've exceeded the limit.
